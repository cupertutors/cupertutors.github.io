"We offer free tutoring in Math, Computer Science, and Chemistry Olympiads."

CuperTutors is a student-led organization from Monta Vista High School that serves to educate students of all ages free of charge. After noticing the dearth of educators that value education over profit, we have created the nonprofit organization, CuperTutors, to educate children in various Olympiads and competitions. Because we have the necessary tools and knowledge, our students thrive under our guidance and achieve their academic goals, becoming the best students they can be.
